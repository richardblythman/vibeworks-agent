## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn
- **Length**: 1-3 paragraphs, up to 3000 characters
- **Tone**: Professional yet conversational, thought leadership
- **Format**: Short paragraphs, line breaks for readability
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant hashtags at the end
- **Best for**: Industry insights, career wins, professional stories, business updates

### Twitter/X
- **Length**: Single tweet (280 chars) or thread (3-10 tweets)
- **Tone**: Conversational, witty, punchy
- **Format**: Short sentences, line breaks, minimal hashtags (0-2)
- **Best for**: Hot takes, quick tips, announcements, threads for deep dives

### Instagram
- **Length**: Caption up to 2200 characters (first 125 visible)
- **Tone**: Authentic, visual-focused, storytelling
- **Format**: Hook in first line, emojis encouraged, hashtags (5-15) in comment or end
- **Best for**: Behind-the-scenes, personal stories, visual content descriptions

### Facebook
- **Length**: 40-80 characters for max engagement (but can be longer)
- **Tone**: Conversational, community-focused, shareable
- **Format**: Questions work well, can include links
- **Best for**: Community posts, event announcements, shareable content

### Threads
- **Length**: 500 characters per post
- **Tone**: Casual, conversational, authentic
- **Format**: Similar to Twitter but more relaxed
- **Best for**: Casual updates, conversations, quick thoughts

## Writing Style Guidelines

### DO âœ…
- **Be authentic** - Share real experiences and genuine reactions
- **Provide value** - Teach something, inspire, or entertain
- **Use specifics** - "10 minutes" beats "quickly"
- **Show personality** - Your unique voice makes content memorable
- **Ask questions** - Invite engagement and conversation
- **Use formatting** - Line breaks, bullets, emojis (platform-appropriate)

### DON'T âŒ
- **Sound like a robot** - No corporate jargon or marketing speak
- **Oversell** - Authentic > promotional
- **Use clickbait** - Deliver on your hook's promise
- **Hashtag spam** - Quality over quantity
- **Be vague** - Specifics create credibility
- **Copy others** - Add your unique perspective

## Engagement Boosters

### Questions That Work
- "What's your experience with [topic]?"
- "Anyone else notice this?"
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Agree or disagree?"
- "What's your take?"

### Formatting Tips
- **Line breaks** make posts scannable
- **Emojis** add personality (use sparingly on LinkedIn, more on Instagram)
- **Numbers** attract attention ("5 things I learned...")
- **Bold statements** stop the scroll
- **Lists** are easy to consume
